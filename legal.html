  • To show you relevant property listings in Nagpur.

🔗 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share only in these cases:

Flatzy uses browser local storage to remember your login session and preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

🔐 5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption. We use Google Sign-In so no passwords are stored on our servers.
