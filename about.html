About Flatzy

Flatzy is a student-led startup making renting simple, transparent, and stress-free for everyone in Nagpur.

Our Story

Flatzy was born from the vision of Sarvadnya Bhoyar, a Computer Science student at YCCE Nagpur. As a student himself, he experienced the struggles of finding quality rental properties — confusing listings, poor communication, and hidden broker fees made the process stressful.

Determined to solve this problem, he built a platform focused on verified listings, user trust, and customer-first support. What started as a college project has now grown into a startup helping people across Nagpur find homes easily and confidently.

Our story is one of passion, innovation, and the belief that technology can make the rental experience smoother, safer, and more reliable for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the rental experience by providing a transparent, reliable, and user-friendly platform that connects people with their perfect homes. We're committed to building trust, fostering community, and making quality housing accessible to everyone.
